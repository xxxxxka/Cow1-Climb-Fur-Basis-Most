Who is Xurrka?

Xurrka is a talented 16-year-old artist from Cleveland, Ohio, recognized for creating rich, atmospheric music that weaves compelling stories. As a self-producer, he meticulously crafts every sound, blending electronic, hip-hop, and ambient elements to create immersive experiences that resonate deeply with listeners. Each track embodies his artistic vision and personal journey, reflecting his ambition to pave a brighter path for his family through his creativity.

With a passion for experimentation and creativity, Xurrka aims to redefine the boundaries of music and inspire his audience.
