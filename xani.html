Who is Xani?

Xani is a creative 17-year-old music producer from Show Low, Arizona, celebrated for his unique soundscapes and boundless vision. His music fuses electronic, trap, and lo-fi elements, capturing listeners with beats that tell stories from his life and world. By blending these genres, Xani creates an immersive vibe that is both fresh and introspective, a style that defies convention and reflects his drive to stand out. Each track he makes represents his journey, a path he hopes to elevate for his family and listeners. Committed to growth, Xani sees his craft as both art and inspiration, demonstrating what is possible at his age.

With a deep passion for music and a drive to experiment, Xani is pushing the boundaries of production, constantly learning and evolving his craft.
