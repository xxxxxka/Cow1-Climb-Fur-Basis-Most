Who is 1slug?

Downfxxl, a 17-year-old artist from Queens, New York, brings raw energy and authenticity to his music, crafting tracks that reflect his experiences and ambitions. Known for his distinct style in jerk music, he explores rap’s versatility, pushing the boundaries of sound and expression. Inspired by mentor Damiku, who played a key role in his first tracks, Downfxxl’s journey is one of constant growth, driven by a dedication to refining his craft and experimenting with new flows. Each song he releases represents a step toward something greater, with endless potential ahead.
