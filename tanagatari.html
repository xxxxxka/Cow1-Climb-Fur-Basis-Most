Who is Tanagatari?

Tanagatari, also known as Maj, is a 17-year-old producer and artist from North Jersey. He crafts captivating soundscapes that reflect his unique perspective, blending various genres to create fresh, innovative music. With a keen ear for detail and an instinct for rhythm, Tanagatari infuses his tracks with a vibrant energy that sets him apart in the music scene. His work often explores themes of identity and personal growth, inviting listeners to embark on a journey through his sonic landscapes. Passionate about collaboration, he actively seeks to connect with other artists, aiming to elevate the local music community and push creative boundaries.
